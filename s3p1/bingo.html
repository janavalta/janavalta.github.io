WILD BINGO

How to Play:

  • Play as a group to complete as many squares in under 5 minutes. Or play for as long as you like.
  • Pick one person to start.
  • A chance is completed if the person picking it matches the prompt/answers the question.
  • Popcorn to another person to play the next square of choice.
